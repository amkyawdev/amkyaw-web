# AmkyawDev User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to AmkyawDev! This guide will help you use our services.

## Browsing Templates

1. Go to the Templates page
2. Use filters to find what you need
3. Click on a template to see details
4. Click "View Demo" to see the live template

## Downloading Templates

1. Create an account or login
2. Browse templates
3. Click "Download" on desired template
4. The template will be downloaded

## Contact Form

1. Go to Contact page
2. Fill in your details
3. Select a subject
4. Write your message
5. Click "Send Message"

## Dashboard

After logging in, you can access your dashboard to:
- View downloaded templates
- See your favorites
- Check notifications
- Update your profile
